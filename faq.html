Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DTF Editor

Image Processing

What file formats are supported?

We support most common image formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, TIFF, and WebP. For vectorization, we recommend high-quality images for best results.

What is the maximum file size?

The maximum file size is 30MB per image. For best performance, we recommend keeping files under 10MB.

How long does processing take?

Processing time varies based on image complexity and size. Simple images typically process in 10-30 seconds, while complex images may take 1-2 minutes.

What's the difference between vectorization and background removal?

Vectorization converts raster images into scalable vector graphics (SVG) that can be resized without quality loss. Background removal creates transparent backgrounds for easier integration into designs.

Storage & Retention

How long are my images stored?

Our image retention policy varies based on your subscription tier:

  • Free Tier: Images are automatically deleted after 7 days
  • Active Paid Subscriptions: Images are stored indefinitely with no automatic deletion
  • Expired Subscriptions: Images are deleted 30 days after subscription expiration

We recommend downloading your processed images to ensure you don't lose them. You can always re-upload and process images again.

Can I download my images?

Yes! You can download your processed images from your dashboard. Vectorized images are downloaded as SVG files, and background-removed images are typically PNG with transparency.

Is there a limit on how many images I can process?

Image processing is limited by your credit balance. Each operation costs credits based on the service used. Free users get 5 credits to start, and paid plans include more credits.

Credits & Billing

How do credits work?

Credits are consumed for each image processing operation. Vectorization typically costs 1 credit, while background removal costs 1 credit. Credits are included with your subscription plan.

Can I purchase additional credits?

Yes, you can purchase additional credits or upgrade your subscription plan to get more credits. Visit your dashboard to manage your subscription.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards through our secure Stripe payment processing. All payments are encrypted and secure.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. Your images will be retained for 30 days after cancellation.

Account & Security

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take security seriously. All images are encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account from your dashboard settings. This will permanently delete all your data, including processed images.

Do you share my images with third parties?

No, we never share your images with third parties. Your images are processed for your use only and are not used for any other purpose.
